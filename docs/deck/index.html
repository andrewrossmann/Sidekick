Sidekick Problem

Life is not flat

Curbs, thresholds, and inclines meet devices that are often too wide, too heavy, or too unstable for real-world confidence.

Walkers & rollators

Wide, cumbersome, or prone to runaway motion on slopes and ramps. Heavy to lift and awkward in tight places.

Canes

Portable, but limited support on uneven ground and for tasks that need both balance and use of the body.

Sidekick Solution

Sidekick: mobile support bar

A portable grab bar, walking aid, and carrier — a step up from a cane.

  • Roll control / resistance — so support stays with you, not away from you
  • Braking approach designed for one-handed, confident use
  • Light and foldable for car, bus, and travel
  • Posture-friendly, substantial hand hold; use beside or in front of the body
Sidekick Emotional & social

Look capable, feel secure

Reduce fear of a runaway rollator or a falling cane. Project agency and poise in restaurants, theaters, family gatherings, and travel — not frailty.

Designed by users

Inventors and partners living with MS, Parkinson’s, and related conditions — building what they would actually use.

Sidekick Everywhere

At home, at work, at play

With family and friends — cooking, cleaning, gardening, projects — and out in the world: shopping, places of worship, sports, airports.

  • Narrow aisles, restrooms, and seating rows
  • Stow on a cart; carry bags; hands available when you need them
  • Get up from chairs with a stable, wide grip
