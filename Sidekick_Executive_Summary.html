Sidekick
Executive summary
Confidential

One-handed support between a cane and a rollator

A portable mobile support bar with roll control, brakes, and a compact fold — for people who need more than a cane and less than a full walker or rollator.

Opportunity

Millions of adults seek stability for walking, standing, and daily tasks, yet many reject bulky walkers or heavy rollators. The gap is a light, maneuverable, dignified device that goes where canes falter and rollators cannot — including narrow venues, curbs, and home activities.

Product

Sidekick (mobile support bar) delivers one-handed support, anti-runaway roll behavior, braking options, and a footprint suited to travel. The roadmap includes Step and Carry (step / tray) and accessories (tray, cupholder, hooks, reach tools) for repeat purchase and online merchandising.

Why now Aging in place, active lifestyles, and demand for “capable” mobility products favor a new category straddling cane and rollator.
Why us Inventors and operators with deep product, manufacturing, and patent experience — including lived experience of the conditions we serve.

Business model

  • Revenue — Direct and online retail with accessible MSRP; accessories; future variants.
  • Strategic — License or sell to a category leader after proof (aligned with M&M Partners’ historical approach).
  • Economics (planning) — Indicative COGS near ~$20 at scale; MSRP $79.99 – $129.99 in source materials (to be validated with BOM and channel).

Team

Cross-functional squad covering product, manufacturing, care settings, community, media, and regulatory advisors — including Rick Mazursky (product realization, PDQ Mazoo, extensive patents and industry leadership) and Mark Williams, with connections to Steve Powell (Indeelift) and engaged clinical and community contributors.

Status & IP

Development is active; patents are pending on key mechanics. Next milestones: finalize BOM, testing, packaging and instructions, and prepare retail / campaign readiness (including any crowdfunding option with clear legal, pricing, and fulfillment plans).
